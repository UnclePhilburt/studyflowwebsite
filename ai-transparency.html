AI Transparency Statement

How StudyFlow Suite uses artificial intelligence, and what we do to keep it honest

AI Disclosure Missouri SB 1324

StudyFlow Suite uses Google Gemini 3 Flash Lite, a large language model, to synthesize and explain content from student-uploaded notes. When you ask a question in our AI Chat, the model generates a response based on material in the Nexus (our shared knowledge base) and your personal uploads.

All AI-generated content on StudyFlow is labeled and watermarked for provenance tracking. This means every response can be traced back to the model, timestamp, and source materials that produced it -- as required by Missouri's AI Transparency and Accountability Act (SB 1324), effective August 28, 2026.

Provenance Markers

Every AI-generated response on StudyFlow carries multiple layers of provenance information so that AI content can always be identified and verified:

  • + Visible Label -- Every AI response displays an "AI-Generated by StudyFlow Suite" label with the exact UTC timestamp of generation.
  • + Depiction Disclaimer -- A notice stating that AI-generated content does not reflect the actual statements or actions of any depicted individual.
  • + Invisible Watermark -- A zero-width character steganography signature is embedded in every AI response, encoding the origin platform and generation timestamp. This watermark persists when text is copied or shared.
  • + Clipboard Attribution -- When AI-generated text is copied from StudyFlow, provenance attribution is automatically appended to the clipboard content.
  • + Source Attribution -- Each response cites the Nexus notes, Wikipedia articles, or other materials that informed the AI's answer, including contributor usernames where available.

Usage Logging & Retention SB 1324 Section 3(b)

As required by Missouri law, StudyFlow maintains encrypted logs of every AI-generated response for a period of seven (7) years. These logs include:

  • +Timestamp of when the response was generated
  • +User identity (who triggered the AI)
  • +The prompt or question that was asked
  • +Output description (response length, cryptographic hash, type)
  • +Sources used (which notes and materials informed the AI)
  • +Model identifier (which AI model generated the response)
  • +Provenance signature (the steganographic watermark embedded in the response)
Your Privacy: While we retain provenance metadata for legal compliance, we do not store the full text of AI responses in these logs. Instead, we store a SHA-256 cryptographic hash that can verify the content without exposing it. For details on how your data is handled, see our Privacy Policy.

Limitations of AI

AI-generated content on StudyFlow is a study aid, not a substitute for academic work. The AI may produce responses that are incomplete, inaccurate, or misleading. Students should always verify AI-generated information against primary sources and exercise their own judgment.

StudyFlow does not use AI to generate or complete academic assignments, write essays for submission, or produce content intended to be presented as a student's own original work. Our AI is designed to explain and synthesize material that students have already uploaded.

Important: StudyFlow Suite is not responsible for academic consequences arising from the use of AI-generated content. Students are responsible for ensuring their use of AI tools complies with their institution's academic integrity policies.
