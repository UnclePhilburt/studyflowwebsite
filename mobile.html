❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I install it on mobile? ▼
StudyFlow is a Chrome browser extension, and Chrome extensions can only be installed on desktop/laptop computers. This is a Chrome limitation, not ours. However, you can email yourself the link and install it on any desktop or laptop (Mac or Windows).
How much does it cost? ▼
StudyFlow is free to try with 10 questions per day - perfect for testing it out. If you need more, the Pro plan is $4.99/month and includes unlimited questions, all question types (essay, fill-in-blank, multiple choice), and priority support.
What platforms does it work with? ▼
StudyFlow works with Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, McGraw Hill Connect, Pearson MyLab, Cengage WebAssign, Top Hat, and 100+ other learning platforms. If your school uses online quizzes, chances are StudyFlow supports it.
Is it safe and secure? ▼
Absolutely. StudyFlow runs locally in your Chrome browser. We use bank-level encryption to protect your data, and we never store your quiz answers or personally identifiable information on our servers. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.
Can I try it before paying? ▼
Yes! The free plan includes 10 questions per day with no credit card required. This lets you test StudyFlow with real quizzes before deciding to upgrade. You can try it risk-free.
