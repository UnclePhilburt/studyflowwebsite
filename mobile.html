FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about StudyFlow

Why can't I install it on my phone? ▼
StudyFlow is a Chrome browser extension. Chrome extensions can only be installed on desktop or laptop computers - this is a Chrome platform limitation, not a StudyFlow restriction. You can email yourself the link and install it on any Mac or Windows computer.
How much does it cost? ▼
StudyFlow offers a free plan with daily usage limits - perfect for testing it out. The Pro plan costs $4.99/month and includes unlimited access, full feature set, and priority support.
What platforms are supported? ▼
StudyFlow works with Canvas, McGraw Hill Connect, Pearson MyLab, Cengage WebAssign, Top Hat, Wiley, and 100+ other learning management systems. If your school uses online quizzes, chances are StudyFlow supports it.
Is my data secure? ▼
Absolutely. StudyFlow runs entirely in your Chrome browser using bank-level encryption. We never store your coursework content or personally identifiable information on our servers. Your privacy and security are our highest priorities.
Can I try it for free? ▼
Yes! The free plan includes daily usage limits with no credit card required. This lets you test StudyFlow with your actual coursework on your learning platform before deciding to upgrade. Try it completely risk-free.
