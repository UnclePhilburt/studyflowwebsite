Common Questions

Everything you need to know about StudyFlow

Why can't I install it on mobile? ▼
StudyFlow is a Chrome browser extension. Chrome extensions can only be installed on desktop or laptop computers - this is a Chrome platform limitation. However, you can email yourself the link and install it on any Mac or Windows computer.
How much does it cost? ▼
StudyFlow offers a free plan with 10 questions per day - perfect for testing it out. The Pro plan costs $4.99/month and includes unlimited questions, all question types (multiple choice, essay, fill-in-blank), and priority support.
What learning platforms are supported? ▼
StudyFlow works with Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, McGraw Hill Connect, Pearson MyLab, Cengage WebAssign, Top Hat, Wiley, and 100+ other learning management systems. If your school uses online quizzes, chances are StudyFlow supports it.
Is my data safe and secure? ▼
Absolutely. StudyFlow runs entirely in your Chrome browser using bank-level encryption. We never store your quiz answers or personally identifiable information on our servers. Your privacy and security are our highest priorities.
Can I try it for free first? ▼
Yes! The free plan includes 10 questions per day with no credit card required. This lets you test StudyFlow with real quizzes on your learning platform before deciding to upgrade. Try it completely risk-free.
