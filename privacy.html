Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to StudyFlow. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

By using StudyFlow, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide and improve our service:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (encrypted) when you create an account
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe - we do not store credit card details
  • Usage Data: Number of questions answered, feature usage, and error logs to improve service quality
  • Technical Data: Browser type, extension version, and basic device information
  • Question Data: Quiz questions, answer choices, and AI-generated answers are stored to improve our service, provide analytics, and enable caching for better performance
Important: We store quiz questions and AI-generated answers to improve our service and provide better performance through caching. This data is used exclusively for service improvement, analytics, and optimizing our AI models. We do not share this data with third parties except as required for AI processing (OpenAI). You can request deletion of your data at any time.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Process your subscription and payments
  • Send you service updates and important notifications
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Improve our AI models and features based on usage patterns
  • Analyze question data to identify common topics and improve answer accuracy
  • Cache frequently asked questions for faster response times
  • Generate analytics and insights on service usage
  • Detect and prevent technical issues or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to user data, and only when necessary for support or maintenance
  • Third-Party Security: We use trusted providers (Render for hosting, Stripe for payments) with strong security practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • OpenAI: AI processing for quiz answers and explanations (quiz content is not stored)
  • Render: Cloud hosting and infrastructure

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after deletion (for recovery purposes)
  • Question Data: Stored indefinitely for service improvement and caching, but can be deleted upon request
  • Usage Statistics: Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for analytics

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails (service emails still required)

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@studyflowsuite.com

8. Children's Privacy

StudyFlow is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification to your registered email address
  • Displaying a notification in the extension

Continued use of StudyFlow after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
