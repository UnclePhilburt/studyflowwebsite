Age & Safety Standards

How StudyFlow Suite verifies user age and protects minors from AI interaction

Age Requirement Missouri SB 1455 / GUARD Act

StudyFlow Suite is designed for college and university students. In compliance with Missouri's GUARD Act (SB 1455), all users must be 18 years of age or older to create an account and interact with our AI-powered features.

Missouri's GUARD Act requires AI chatbot operators to implement robust age verification processes to prevent minors from interacting with AI companions and conversational AI systems. StudyFlow takes this obligation seriously and has implemented a multi-step verification process.

Legal Notice: Under Missouri SB 1455, operators who knowingly allow minors to interact with AI chatbot systems without proper age verification may face penalties of up to $100,000 per violation. StudyFlow enforces strict age verification to comply with this requirement and protect younger users.

How We Verify Age

StudyFlow uses a tiered verification approach during account registration to confirm that all users meet the minimum age requirement:

  1. .edu Email Verification -- Users register with a valid .edu email address issued by an accredited college or university. Since .edu domains are only issued to post-secondary institutions, this serves as a strong initial indicator that the user is of college age (18+).
  2. Email Confirmation -- A verification link is sent to the .edu email address. The user must click this link from within the institution's email system to confirm ownership, preventing the use of fabricated or borrowed email addresses.
  3. Terms Acceptance -- During registration, users must affirmatively confirm that they are 18 years of age or older and agree to our Terms of Service, which include the age requirement.
  4. Government ID Verification (if required) -- In cases where .edu email verification is insufficient or unavailable, StudyFlow may require government-issued photo ID verification to confirm the user's age before granting access to AI features.

If You Are Under 18

If you are under 18 years old, you are not eligible to use StudyFlow Suite. We do not knowingly collect personal information from or provide AI services to minors.

If we discover that an account belongs to a user under 18, we will:

  • Immediately suspend the account and revoke access to all AI features
  • Delete any personal data associated with the account
  • Delete any AI interaction logs associated with the account
  • Notify the user (and their parent or guardian, if contact information is available)

Notice to Parents & Guardians

StudyFlow Suite includes an AI-powered chat feature that uses Google Gemini to generate responses based on student-uploaded notes. While this tool is educational in nature, Missouri law requires that only verified adults (18+) interact with AI chatbot systems.

If you believe your child has created an account on StudyFlow without meeting the age requirement, please contact us immediately and we will remove the account and all associated data.

Report Underage Account: safety@studyflowsuite.com

Data Protection for Young Adults

Even for verified users aged 18+, StudyFlow applies strong data protection practices:

  • We do not sell personal data to third parties
  • AI interaction data is logged for legal compliance only (see our AI Transparency Statement)
  • Users can request deletion of their data at any time (see our Privacy Policy)
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
