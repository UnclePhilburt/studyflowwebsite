Academic Integrity Commitment

StudyFlow Suite is a tutorial and research tool built to support honest learning

A Message to Professors & Administrators Missouri HB 2271

We understand the concerns that educators and institutions have about AI-powered study tools. StudyFlow Suite was built by students, for students -- and we share your commitment to academic integrity.

StudyFlow is not a commercial cheating service. It does not write essays, complete assignments, take exams, or produce work intended to be submitted as a student's own. Missouri's Commercial Cheating Services Act (HB 2271) targets services that facilitate plagiarism and academic dishonesty. StudyFlow was designed from the ground up to fall outside that scope.

Our platform is a tutorial and research tool that helps students understand material they have already encountered in class. Students upload their own notes, and our AI helps them review, synthesize, and ask questions about that content -- the same way a study group or tutoring session would.

Built-In Safeguards

StudyFlow has multiple technical safeguards in place to prevent academic dishonesty and ensure our platform is used responsibly:

Flattened PDF Downloads

All note downloads are flattened PDFs with embedded watermarks. Text cannot be selected, copied, or pasted from downloaded files, preventing direct plagiarism.

Watermark Attribution

Every downloaded file includes a visible watermark with the downloader's username and a unique transaction ID, creating a traceable chain of custody.

AI Provenance Markers

All AI-generated text carries visible labels, invisible steganographic watermarks, and clipboard attribution -- making it identifiable even when copied elsewhere.

Download Limits

Users are limited to 3 downloads per day and must contribute at least 5 notes before downloading, ensuring the platform rewards genuine participation.

What StudyFlow Is -- and Isn't

StudyFlow IS:
  • A note-sharing platform where students upload their own study materials
  • An AI tutor that explains and synthesizes content students have already encountered
  • A collaborative study tool (the Nexus) that connects learners across universities
  • A research aid that cites sources, including Wikipedia articles with proper CC BY-SA attribution
StudyFlow IS NOT:
  • An essay-writing or assignment-completion service
  • A platform for sharing exam questions, answer keys, or proprietary course materials
  • A tool that produces work intended to be submitted as a student's own
  • A service that circumvents academic integrity detection systems

Prohibited Content

The following materials are strictly prohibited on StudyFlow and will be removed immediately upon discovery:

  • Scanned or photographed textbook pages (copyrighted material)
  • Exam questions, quizzes, or answer keys from any institution
  • Homework assignments or lab reports intended for submission
  • Proprietary course materials (syllabi marked "Do Not Distribute," instructor slides, etc.)
  • Any content uploaded for the purpose of facilitating academic dishonesty

Users who repeatedly upload prohibited content will have their accounts permanently suspended. See our Community Guidelines for full details.
