About StudyFlow Suite

An all-in-one study platform built to help students learn smarter with AI-powered tools and a shared knowledge network.

The Mission

StudyFlow Suite exists because studying shouldn't mean struggling alone. Students juggle notes across classes, formats, and platforms -- and when it's time to actually learn the material, the tools available either don't understand your content or don't work together.

StudyFlow Suite changes that. Upload your notes -- PDFs, images, docs, text files -- and chat with an AI that reads and understands your actual coursework. Ask questions, get explanations, and study with answers grounded in your own material. No generic responses, no hallucinated facts.

Need to create or edit documents? The Private Office gives you a full office suite right in your browser -- Word documents, spreadsheets, and presentations -- plus a built-in PDF editor for annotating and modifying your study materials. Everything saves securely to your personal cloud storage.

Beyond personal study, the Nexus connects students across universities through a shared library of notes. Browse materials from other students, filter by school and course, and contribute to a growing knowledge network that makes everyone's studying more effective.

How It Works

Upload Your Notes

Drop in your PDFs, images, Word docs, or text files. StudyFlow processes and indexes everything so the AI can reference it.

Chat With Your Material

Ask questions, get explanations, quiz yourself, or have the AI summarize key concepts -- all based on your actual notes.

Share to the Nexus

Opt in to share your notes publicly. Browse materials from other students, filter by university and course, and learn together.

Edit Documents

Create and edit Word docs, spreadsheets, and presentations in the Private Office. Annotate PDFs with the built-in PDF editor. All saved to your cloud.

What We Believe

Students First

Every feature exists to solve a real problem students face. The platform is shaped by feedback and built around what actually helps people learn.

Your Data, Your Control

Notes are private by default. You choose what to share. We use .edu verification to keep the Nexus a trusted academic space.

Knowledge Is Collective

The best study tool isn't one student's notes -- it's thousands of students helping each other. The Nexus makes that real.

Honest & Transparent

No inflated stats, no fake promises. We're upfront about what the AI can and can't do, and we grow based on real feedback.
