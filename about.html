About StudyFlow Suite

A student-built platform that turns your notes into an AI-powered study companion and connects you with a living network of shared knowledge.

Our Story

StudyFlow Suite started with a frustration every student knows: you have stacks of notes, but when it comes time to study, you're on your own sifting through pages trying to find what matters. We wanted something smarter.

So we built it. StudyFlow Suite lets you upload your notes and chat with an AI that actually understands your material. It reads your PDFs, images, docs, and text files, then gives you real answers grounded in your own coursework. No generic responses, no hallucinated facts -- just your notes, made conversational.

But we didn't stop there. We created the Nexus: a shared library where students across universities can discover and learn from each other's materials. When you share your notes, they become part of a growing knowledge network that helps everyone study smarter.

How It Works

Upload Your Notes

Drop in your PDFs, images, Word docs, or text files. StudyFlow processes and indexes everything so the AI can reference it.

Chat With Your Material

Ask questions, get explanations, quiz yourself, or have the AI summarize key concepts -- all based on your actual notes.

Share to the Nexus

Opt in to share your notes publicly. Browse materials from other students, filter by university and course, and learn together.

What We Believe

Students First

Every feature is built because students asked for it. We use our own product every day and build what we wish existed.

Your Data, Your Control

Notes are private by default. You choose what to share. We use .edu verification to keep the Nexus a trusted academic space.

Knowledge Is Collective

The best study tool isn't one student's notes -- it's thousands of students helping each other. The Nexus makes that real.

Honest & Transparent

No inflated stats, no fake promises. We're upfront about what the AI can and can't do, and we grow based on real feedback.
