Community Guidelines

To keep the Nexus safe, legal, and helpful for everyone, all uploads must follow these rules. By uploading content to StudyFlow, you agree to these community standards.

1
No copyrighted textbooks or exams
Your personal notes are fine—published books, exam keys, and copyrighted materials are not.
✓ Your handwritten notes from a textbook chapter
✗ Scanned pages from the actual textbook
2
No hate speech or harassment
Keep it professional and respectful. StudyFlow is a collaborative learning environment for everyone.
✓ Collaborative study notes and helpful explanations
✗ Offensive language, slurs, or targeted harassment
3
No Personal Identifiable Information (PII)
Remove SSNs, credit cards, addresses, and other sensitive information before uploading.
✓ Notes with your initials or first name
✗ Documents containing full SSN, address, or financial data
4
Academic integrity only
Use StudyFlow as a study aid, not to cheat. Respect your institution's honor code and academic policies.
✓ Using StudyFlow to prepare for exams and understand concepts
✗ Uploading current exam questions or using AI to complete graded assignments
StudyFlow LLC reserves the right to remove any content that violates these guidelines, Missouri state law (SB 1324, SB 1455), or federal regulations. Repeated violations may result in account suspension.
